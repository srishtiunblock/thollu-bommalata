Kathakalp

KathaKalp is a multiplayer educational game designed for 8-9 year olds. It has been designed as a curriculum (hypothetical) that can be followed in schools with teachers acting as facilitators. This game indulges students in the realm of obstacles, objectives, choices and characters. They learn morals and values through a fun method of storytelling and performance.
