Performance 1 - The Tree of Life


The nature of our future depends on the future of our nature.

Final Output

  • The script of the performance
  • Character designs of the puppets.
  • Posters and social media posts
  • 3d render/animation, layering on existing footages

Design Process / Approach

We first started with the script writing process. Our aim was to show an environmental issue through this. We edited the script several times and arrived at the final one. Then the characters of the script were developed and created inspired by the Tholu Bommalata art style. Then using real footage they were rendered on the software, and the outcome is a 3D animation. We are making posters and social media posts, as part of outreach.

Future Roadmap

1. We wanted to create a space where we could engage and collaborate with the Tholu Bommalata artisans from Nimmalakunta by creating 3d render/animation which provides access in a virtual platform for performing the artform in CGI. If someone else were to take it ahead we would suggest that they focus on the movement of the puppet the way it moves in live performance for a better understanding of the story in the virtual space. Hence, he/she can mimic the live performance in CGI.

2. We wanted to enhance the performance by adding layers/backgrounds on the existing footages which makes it look as if it has been projected from the back of the screen. If someone else were to take it ahead then we would suggest that they focus on blending different layers with the footage to achieve realism as much as possible.
