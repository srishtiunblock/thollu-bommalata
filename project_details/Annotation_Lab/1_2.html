Poralu


Layering of Time and History


Design process/approach

Initially, we were swamped with a lot of knowledge about Tholu Bommalata. We looked into the history of the artform and how it started. Coming up with various ideas, we started ideating in the form of sketches, GIFs, short videos and animations.







Parts to Highlight

Future Roadmap

The intention is to keep spreading awareness about the art form by giving minor details of the history of tholu bommalata in interactive GIFs/videos. For now it is a complete project, but it can be converted in a capstone project in the future. If anyone else were to take up this project they could look into more about the rich history and culture of this art form and community.
