Parts to highlight

  1. Spreading awareness about Tholu Bommatala by creating interactive posts.
  2. To give a boost to the artisans by giving different perspectives and generating new ideas.
  3. To promote “Make in India”

Future Roadmap

  1. To engage with art lovers, professionals and mass audiences through social media by keeping the authenticity of the craft intact and opening up further possibilities.
  2. Create an active community of artists, and enthusiasts by sharing our experience about the endless opportunities the craft entails.
  3. Create crowdfunding campaigns for the education of the coming generations of tholu artisans (charm chitrakaras) by initiating creative campaigns to create awareness.
  4. Reach a larger audience through interactive videos about the richness of craft and its possibilities.
  5. Short film on Tholu Bommalata artists to effectively engage a more widespread community.
