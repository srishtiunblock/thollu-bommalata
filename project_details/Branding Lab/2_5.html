Parts to highlight

  1. To keep the ancient culture of spreading knowledge about epics by showing more interactive outputs of the crafts.
  2. To encourage people to use natural leather by incorporating it in products of daily use.
  3. To boost the economy of that community by innovating their production with new ideas.
  4. The color scheme and the authentic design keeps the essence of the brand and the craft intact.

Future Roadmap

  1. To spread awareness about the products being made with this rich art form from a single region to all over the country and taking it further globally through our website and social media.
  2. To familiarise the coming generations about this craft through products and its making processes.
  3. To uplift the artisans and their whole community economically by giving them substantial support and enabling them to gain a global recognition.
  4. To reignite and re-establish the dying art form.
