Ideation

We all made a miro board, and brainstormed our ideas into it. And narrowed down to 1 Idea.
Below are some initial ideations of the board :



The game was to have a connection with tholu bommalata, so our team decided to design a game from one of the epics portrayed in the craft, namely Ramayana. Ramayana having 36 main characters, many of which were finalised for the game.

Equal number of characters devas and rakshasa were chosen in order to have a fair game. So, we chose between these characters.

  • DEVAS TEAM: Ram, Lakshman, Sita, Hanuman, Anjaneya, Ganesha
  • RAKSHASA TEAM: Surpankha, Kumbhkarana, Ravana, Lankini, Kumbhkaran, Rakshas
  • NEUTRAL: Elephant, Horse, Deer

Final Game






If a player lands on a space with an animal card he/she has to perform dares to be a part of the game. The dare should be given by the player nearest their place. Specific set of rules has to be set by the players on the nature of the dares which must be followed.

Future Roadmap

The game has scope for future development in a physical(table-top) form and digital format. The timeline of 3 weeks has given us space for less possibility and the team is open to hear ideas from others which might improve the game. The thrill quotient and overall aspects of the game can be modified to be apt for the young generation. The game’s idea also has the potential to enter the digital gaming field in the future.
