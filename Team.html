Shadang

Given the current situation and the reality of us being spread across the country, it was known that Unblock this year was going to be a tough task. Understanding the physical challenges we realised how important it really was that the projects created had good support and visibility. This is where the interactivity team jumped in. We decided to create a website not just for this year's project but also for Unblock as an event which can be used by the future batches. The project wiki was made in a way that would fit all the different kinds of ideas and outputs that were produced.

Although we did not work directly with the artisans, this is our small contribution to the community - ensuring that the projects created have visibility and reach a wide audience.
